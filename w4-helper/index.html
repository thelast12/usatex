Free U.S. Salary Tax Calculator

TaxCalc USA is a free payroll and salary tax calculator for all 50 U.S. states. Enter your annual salary, hourly wage, or monthly income and instantly see your federal income tax, state income tax, Social Security, Medicare, and exact take-home pay — updated for 2026 IRS tax brackets.

What Can You Calculate?

  • 📊 Paycheck Calculator — net pay per pay period
  • ⏰ Overtime Pay Calculator — FLSA 1.5× and double time
  • 🏢 Self-Employment Tax — SE tax + deductions
  • 💸 Tax Refund Estimator — federal refund or amount owed
  • 🗺 State Tax Comparison — all 50 states ranked by take-home pay
  • 🔄 Salary to Hourly Converter — all pay periods

How Are Taxes Calculated?

Your gross income is reduced by the standard deduction (or itemized), then taxed using progressive 2026 federal tax brackets. On top of federal income tax, we calculate state income tax for your selected state, Social Security (6.2%), and Medicare (1.45%). Additional 0.9% Medicare applies above $200,000.

2026 Federal Tax Brackets

  • 10% — up to $11,925 (Single)
  • 12% — $11,926 – $48,475
  • 22% — $48,476 – $103,350
  • 24% — $103,351 – $197,300
  • 32% — $197,301 – $250,525
  • 35% — $250,526 – $626,350
  • 37% — over $626,350

States With No Income Tax

Nine states charge zero state income tax: Alaska, Florida, Nevada, New Hampshire, South Dakota, Tennessee, Texas, Washington, and Wyoming. Use our State Compare tool to see exactly how much more take-home pay you'd earn in a no-tax state.
